Music

Cello a cappella

From kindergarten through eighth grade, I had the interesting experience of attending a public charter school in Manhattan called Special Music School, which aims to provide both an academic and musical education. It was there that I learned music, and specifically, I became skilled at playing the cello. In high school, I continued playing at the Perlman Music Program and at Juilliard's Pre-College program. The following are a few a capella (solo) cello recordings I have made.
