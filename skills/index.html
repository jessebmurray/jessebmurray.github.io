Leadership

In the fall of 2019, I was President of the Math Club at Drew University and completely redesigned the club, which up to that point was only meeting a few times a semester to eat pizza and play chess. I put up posters around the school of interesting math problems, most of them applying math to real-life situations. I started weekly meetings to solve those problems and to provide students the opportunity to lead discussions on their projects/research or a math-related topic they find interesting. I also began a professor talk series in which quantitative professors at Drew give a math-related talk. In my tenure as President, I redesigned the election process to use the Condorcet method of ranked choice voting.
